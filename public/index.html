An open-source carburetor synchronization tool that displays live readings on your phone. No app download. No internet. No long hoses.

See It In Action ↓ How It Works
Setup

Three steps. No app store.

01
Power on
Flip the switch. The tool creates its own WiFi network — no internet needed.
02
Connect to WiFi
Join the CarbSync network from your phone's WiFi settings.
03
Open your browser
Go to 192.168.4.1 and you're looking at live gauges.
Optional: Add it to your home screen for an app-like experience. iPhone: Share → Add to Home Screen. Android: Menu → Install App. It'll sit next to your other apps and open full-screen.
